Clinical Trial Search Engine Case study
  • Problem: Find relevant trials quickly from large scale, noisy eligibility criteria and free text descriptions.
  • Approach: BM25 (OpenSearch) + dense retrieval (PubMedBERT) fused via RRF; AI based feasibility scoring with cached eligibility parsing.
  • Scale: Ingestion pipeline scrapes and normalizes and indexes 560k+ trials into PostgreSQL + OpenSearch; FAISS reranks top candidates.
  • Results: <2s query latency; strong retrieval quality on TREC 2021 Clinical Trials benchmark (MRR@10 0.48, Hit@10 70%).
